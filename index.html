About

Welcome to MOVIFEY

Movifey is a global social network for film discussion and discovery.
Use it to record and share your opinion about films you have watched before,
or just to keep track of films you have seen in the past. Showcase
your favorites on your profile page. Rate, review and tag films as you add them.
Find people who is interested in the same thing that you're enjoying.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked quetions that might help you

  • How do I become a member?
    • Create an account, there is no other way as getting invitation from any member.
  • Does it cost to use this service?
    • No, Movifey will always remain free to use. Some features are only available by upgrading to a Pro Movifey.
  • Will I be paid for my reviews?
    • No, We do not offer payment for reviews, but we are very happy for our members to build their own paying audience via services.
  • How do I close my account?
    • go to your profile then click deactivate account.
